ABOUT US

A academic progress management system is a software application that helps educational institutions manage and maintain student records, grades, and academic progress. The system provides a centralized platform for teachers, administrators, and students to access information related to academic performance. The system typically includes a database that stores student information, such as names, addresses, and contact details, as well as their grades, attendance records, and other relevant information. Overall, a APMS streamlines the process of managing and maintaining student records, enabling educational institutions to better track student progress and communicate with students and parents
